## Paving the way to resolution

Alfred T. Denham has been a Texas trial lawyer since 1983 and a mediator since 1997. Today his McAllen-based practice — the Law Office of Alfred T. Denham — has resolved more than 5,500 cases for plaintiffs' and defense attorneys across personal injury, products liability, employment, insurance, real estate, and civil litigation.

When attorneys need a Valley mediator who can move a difficult case off the docket, Mr. Denham is the call. Justice Nora Longoria — a 34-year legal veteran with twelve years on the Texas 13th Court of Appeals — joined the practice as a second mediator, doubling the firm's capacity without diluting its credibility.

A mediation practice lives or dies on attorney trust. The website had to telegraph that trust the moment the page loaded.

## The challenge: trust at the speed of a referral

Mediation is a referral-driven business. An attorney recommends a mediator to opposing counsel; opposing counsel either accepts in five minutes or doesn't. Whatever the recommended attorney finds when they Google the mediator becomes part of the decision.

The site had to do four jobs in those five minutes: confirm the mediator's pedigree, communicate that this is a serious practice (not a solo shingle), make booking frictionless, and present both Mr. Denham and Justice Longoria with the gravity their backgrounds deserve.

The previous site couldn't do any of those things at the speed an attorney expected. Bookings happened by phone tag. Calendars weren't visible. The depth of the practice wasn't on the page.

## The solution: a referral-grade mediation site

We rebuilt the site as a credibility instrument — designed to convert the five-minute referral check into a confirmed booking.

### Photography that does the work

Professional photography of both mediators, shot in the firm's actual conference rooms. The images communicate what a brochure can't: this is where serious cases get settled.

### Two mediators, two voices

Parallel profile pages for Mr. Denham and Justice Longoria — career, jurisdictions, mediation philosophy, types of cases handled. Attorneys can match the right mediator to the case in one read.

### Calendar-first booking

Custom booking calendar embedded on every page. The "Book with Alfred" and "Book with Nora" CTAs sit above the fold; an attorney can lock in a date without leaving the site or starting a phone tree.

### Modern, mobile-responsive

Designed mobile-first because most referral checks happen on a phone — usually right after a hearing or in a courthouse hallway. Pages load fast, the booking flow works on iOS, and the typography reads as confident across screen sizes.

### Content that ranks

Practice-area content and a content marketing program that puts the firm in front of attorneys searching for Valley mediators. Not consumer SEO — referral SEO.

## The outcome

The new alfredtdenham.com works the way the practice works: quietly, efficiently, and with the gravity of forty years of trial experience behind it. Bookings flow through the site. Attorney referrals confirm in minutes instead of days. The addition of Justice Longoria reads as a deepening of the practice, not a competing brand.

When a Valley attorney needs a mediator who can settle a hard case, Google sends them to a site that closes the loop in five minutes. That's all a mediation site needs to do — and this one does it.
