We’ve been proud to partner with the Children’s Museum of Brownsville for several years, so when it came time for a website redesign, we knew we had to deliver something special. The new site, [CMBKids.org](https://cmbkids.org), is now live, and we think you’re going to love what we’ve done with it.

### **A Site That Captures the Magic of the Museum**

The Children’s Museum of Brownsville is a local treasure, a place where kids can explore, learn, and let their imaginations run wild. The museum’s hands-on exhibits have been sparking curiosity in kids from birth to 8 years old for years. Our goal with the new website was to reflect that same sense of wonder and discovery online.

### **What’s New?**

We’ve packed the new site with features to make visiting the museum easier and more fun:

-   **Buy Tickets Online:** No more waiting in line. Now, you can snag your tickets in advance.
-   **Book Birthday Parties:** Hosting a party at the museum? You can now book everything online, from the date to the theme.
-   **Check Out Events:** Want to catch the next big event like the Teddy Bear Clinic or Pirate Day? The event calendar’s got you covered.
-   **Plan Field Trips:** Teachers, we’ve made it simple to organize a field trip with all the info you need in one spot.
-   **Virtual Exhibit Previews:** Can’t wait to visit? Get a sneak peek at what’s inside with high-quality photos of the exhibits.
-   **Donate with Ease:** Love the museum’s mission? Supporting it is now just a few clicks away.

### **Designed for Everyone**

We designed this site with one goal: to make sure it’s as easy and enjoyable to use as the museum is to visit. Whether you’re on your phone or at your desk, everything works smoothly, looks great, and is just plain fun to explore.

### **A Bit About the Museum**

The Children’s Museum of Brownsville isn’t just a place to play—it’s a space where learning and fun collide. Since opening in 2005, the museum has been dedicated to helping kids learn through interactive experiences. It’s no surprise that the museum has won awards like the Promising Practice Award and been voted Brownsville’s Favorite Museum more than once.

### **Ready to Visit?**

The museum is open Tuesday to Saturday from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., and Sunday from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. Admission is $10, with free entry for kids under 12 months. It’s affordable, it’s fun, and now, with the new website, it’s easier than ever to plan your visit.

### **A Word on Digital Design**

This project was more than just a website update—it’s about enhancing how the museum connects with the community. We believe a well-designed website isn’t just about aesthetics; it’s about making sure the site serves its purpose in the best way possible. For the Children’s Museum of Brownsville, that means making it easier for families to engage with all the museum has to offer.

### **Check It Out**

We invite you to explore the new site at [CMBKids.org](https://cmbkids.org). Whether you’re planning your next visit, booking a birthday party, or just curious about what’s happening at the museum, it’s all there, ready and waiting.

At MPC Studios, we’re thrilled to have brought this new digital experience to life for the Children’s Museum of Brownsville. We can’t wait to see how it helps them continue their mission of inspiring young minds.
