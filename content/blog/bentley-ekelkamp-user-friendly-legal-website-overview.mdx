When it comes to law firms specializing in [estate planning](https://bandelegal.com/), [probate](https://bandelegal.com/), and [elder law](https://bandelegal.com/), first impressions matter—especially online. Bentley and Ekelkamp, a Houston-based law firm, understands this perfectly. Their website, [**bnelegal.com**](http://bnelegal.com), is designed to help families protect what matters most with clarity, professionalism, and ease of use. Let’s take a detailed look at how this site stands out as a model for legal services websites.

## Clear and Purposeful Homepage

From the moment you land on the homepage, it’s immediately clear what Bentley and Ekelkamp offer and how to get in touch. Two distinct calls to action invite visitors to engage right away, whether they’re seeking information or ready to [schedule a consultation](https://bandelegal.com/).

The navigation is impressively clean and simple, ensuring that no matter what visitors are searching for, the answers are just one click away. The site even includes an [online payment system](https://www.clio.com/features/payments/) that integrates securely with [Clio](https://www.clio.com/features/), the firm’s internal CRM. This integration streamlines invoicing, reduces administrative workload, and shortens the collection cycle, benefiting both staff and clients.

![Homepage with clear calls to action and simple navigation](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/0af26ec6-3759-4681-904e-29e668e92f48.png)

## Authentic Imagery Builds Trust

Many law firms fall into the trap of using generic stock photos, but Bentley and Ekelkamp have taken a different route. Their website features actual photos of the team members throughout, which establishes a personal connection and builds trust before the first phone call even happens.

Visitors can see exactly who will be guiding them through their legal journey, reducing uncertainty and fostering confidence in the firm’s expertise.

![Team photos of Bentley and Ekelkamp lawyers](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/9f321fde-6b55-4546-9374-4721fb925509.png)

## Simple, Plain English Practice Areas

The site’s practice areas are presented in clear, straightforward tiles that enable visitors to quickly identify their needs. There’s no legal jargon—just plain English that helps funnel visitors to the right information efficiently.

This thoughtful design means that even those unfamiliar with legal terminology can navigate with ease and understand the services offered.

![Practice area tiles in plain English](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/c6ade85b-243c-4857-bf40-72ef951efb43.png)

## High-Intent Lead Capture with Pre-Consultation Worksheets

Bentley and Ekelkamp offer free pre-consultation worksheets that serve multiple purposes. These worksheets position the firm as helpful and approachable, while also capturing high-intent leads. This dual benefit saves preparation time for both potential clients and the legal team, making the consultation process smoother and more productive.

![Pre-consultation worksheets available for download](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/8d3a7203-9182-4365-a3d7-d07f0ef38dc1.png)

## Comprehensive About Us Section Highlighting Legal Expertise

The About Us page spotlights each partner’s credentials, including board certifications and national designations. These are critical proof points for elder law clients who want to ensure they’re working with knowledgeable and reputable attorneys.

This transparency reassures visitors and strengthens the firm’s credibility.

![About Us page showing partner bios and certifications](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/f19958f6-e7a0-4144-b77a-69268c51f624.png)

## Resourceful FAQ and Questionnaire Funnel

The Resources and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) sections are thoughtfully designed to guide visitors through the legal process. A clear call to action encourages users to complete questionnaires that initiate the client onboarding funnel. Once completed, visitors become more engaged and invested, which is an effective way to generate committed leads.

The FAQ acts as a living library, catering to long-tail search traffic and showcasing the firm’s expertise without distracting from the main conversion goals. Every page includes a prompt to schedule a consultation, keeping the path to becoming a client straightforward and accessible.

![Resources page with FAQs and consultation prompts](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/fed2bba4-a117-4928-aab7-61794d60f237.png)

## Footer with Essential Contact Information and SEO Benefits

In the footer, visitors find two office locations complete with addresses, phone numbers, Google Maps links, and click-to-call functionality. All contact information is hyperlinked, providing quick access for users on mobile devices.

This setup not only enhances user convenience and trust but also boosts the firm’s search engine optimization ([SEO](https://moz.com/learn/seo/what-is-seo)) by clearly associating the website with its geographic locations.

![Footer showing locations, phone numbers, and map links](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/bentley-ekelkamp-user-friendly-legal-website-overview/e3dc9206-f258-4335-aad2-f4c8c95b7881.png)
