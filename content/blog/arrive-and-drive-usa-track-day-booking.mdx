If you’ve ever watched a race and thought, “I want to be in that car,” [Arrive and Drive USA](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/arrive-and-drive) turns that feeling into a reality — and we built the website to make it dead simple. This project from [MPC Studios](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/author/mpc_admin) is an example of professional motorsports website development done with clarity: A clear offer, transparent pricing, and the kind of information people actually need before they show up at the track.

## Homepage — the offer is simple: show up and drive

The homepage gets straight to the point. Big headline, clear call to action: book your track day experience, show up, and drive. The site immediately answers the key question for anyone curious — what do I need to do? — and it builds trust by listing pro-maintained cars, support, and options for every skill level.

![Homepage hero with 'Book your track day experience, show up, and drive' headline](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/f0e88de4-3250-499e-b20d-c3646586ff0c.png)

## Booking flow: Step 1 — choose your event

Booking is split into simple steps so decisions are fast. Step 1 is all about finding the right date and racetrack. The calendar and event listings are built to reduce friction: browse dates, pick a track that fits your schedule, and lock it in.

![Event calendar and track selection interface showing dates and tracks](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/06cd4d29-9fde-4892-b6de-f54166349b2d.png)

## Booking flow: Step 2 — choose your car

Step 2 presents the [fleet](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/arrive-and-drive/reserve) and what’s included for each car. Listings show specifications, day and weekend pricing, and the minimum driver level. That clarity makes it easy to match the right car to your experience — no guessing, no surprises.

![Arrive and Drive fleet page listing cars, specs, and pricing](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/df2b442d-6d9f-498c-a8f1-818f90144d98.png)

## What’s included — full paddock service, insurance, data-rich video, and options

Every package is more than just a car. The baseline includes paddock setup (shade, seating, drinks, snacks), [trackside support](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/services/race-day-track-support) for tires and adjustments, track day insurance with a 10% deductible, and [in-car video](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/services/track-equipment-demos) with a data overlay. You also get an allocation of [150 miles](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/arrive-and-drive) per day, with clear overage pricing if you go beyond that.

![Package inclusions list: paddock setup, trackside support, insurance, in-car video](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/e68638aa-6171-4e9a-9d54-1b6c950bb26b.png)

## Add-ons and logistics — build the day you want

If you want to tailor the experience, the site lists add-ons like scrubbed racing slicks, helmet and HANS rental, and [professional coaching](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/services/driving-simulator-coaching). Fuel and transport costs are handled transparently — fuel is billed after use and transport to out-of-area tracks is quoted as needed. Policies are written in plain language so there are no confusing terms hiding in the fine print.

![Options and add-ons: slicks, helmet rental, coaching, transport notes](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/3c9fcda4-8916-462d-ab68-c584ed450987.png)

## The fleet — something for every level

The fleet is curated to cover multiple entry points. At the top end are purpose-built Porsche GT4 Club Sport competition cars with paddle-shift gearboxes. Step-down options include a Porsche GT4 RS, a 911 Carrera 2S, and Cayman GTS builds. For a friendly starting point there’s a Mini Cooper GP. Each listing specifies drivetrain and transmission so drivers can match car to skill and goals.

![Fleet lineup showing Porsche GT4 Club Sport and other cars](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/c97206ac-9f9b-42cf-acb9-1a13edb5c00f.png)

## Car listing details — specs, pricing, and driver level at a glance

Each vehicle page is formatted to answer immediate questions: what’s the price for a day vs. a weekend, what’s the minimum experience we expect, and what technical specs matter. That layout reduces hesitation and speeds up booking for both first-timers and regulars.

![Individual car listing with day/weekend pricing, minimum driver level, drivetrain](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/7b0c6a20-e6e9-4ac6-bc18-73ef3c6d8d88.png)

## Corporate program — entertain clients the fast way

Arrive and Drive USA offers two [corporate formats](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/corporate-arrive-drive): a lead-follow session for newer drivers with professional coaches, and hot sessions for experienced groups who want faster track time. Both formats include the same hospitality and support — paddock setup, insurance, on-site crew — with optional catering, photography, videography, branded merchandise, and pro ride-alongs.

![Corporate program options including lead-follow and hot sessions](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/87ccf819-6fc2-4099-b085-bc872c5f3b51.png)

## Who this is for — a wide audience

The site calls out the audience clearly: anyone who’s dreamed of driving fast on a real circuit; drivers who’ve done HPDE and want to level up with better hardware and data; teams looking for memorable corporate experiences; and track regulars who value reliability and time saved. That makes the site feel welcoming and practical at once.

![Audience callouts: first-timers, HPDE drivers, teams, track regulars](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/7c05e8aa-d82b-4e4e-a1e1-98eee84668ee.png)

## Contact, account management, and practical details

Practical information is easy to find. The [contact page](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/contact) lists phone, email, and a Birmingham address, while an inquiry form captures essentials so a real person can follow up. Returning customers can use the [My Account](https://arriveanddriveusa.com/my-account) portal to manage bookings and reservations without the usual back-and-forth emails — small features that make a big difference in user experience.

![Contact page showing phone, email, physical address and inquiry form](https://mpcstudios-media.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/mpc-studios/blog/arrive-and-drive-usa-track-day-booking/a157ff98-58e2-4236-828a-d4e9550530dc.png)

## Bottom line — remove the barriers and get you on track

The core promise is simple: remove the barriers that keep most people off track. Arrive and Drive USA handles sourcing, prepping, transporting, and maintaining cars, plus paddock support and troubleshooting. You book the date, pick the car, show up with your helmet, and drive. The site communicates that in straightforward language and with transparent pricing.
