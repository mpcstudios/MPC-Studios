The Financial Brand explores a critical pivot for financial institutions looking to gain leverage in the AI arms race. The conversation is moving away from generic tools and toward a hidden asset most banks are overlooking.

The reality is that relying solely on off-the-shelf AI creates a parity trap where everyone has the same capabilities. The real strategic move is to leverage the learned intelligence of your employees—capturing the specific, nuanced decisions they make daily to build a proprietary model that actually compounds in value. Instead of just automating tasks, the goal is to institutionalize the expertise that already exists within your walls to create an advantage generic models cannot replicate.

How are you thinking about capturing internal knowledge for your AI strategy?
